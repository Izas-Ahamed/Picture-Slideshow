Sliding Images
1.It has 5 images in total.
2.Each image slides in 2000ms delay.
3.You can't skip to previous image while its in 1st image. 
4.You can't skip to next image while its in 5th image(last photo).
5.If you click left arrow button it moves to previous images and start sliding automatically until it reach last image(5th image) also right arrow button for moving to the next image.
